The Hempest (Back Bay)

Location: Boston » Back Bay » Newbury Street

Price Point: $

You don’t have to be a hippie to shop here, but it helps if you’re cool around colorful, blown-glass pipes. Go green with their enormous selection of organic clothing and accessories made from cannabis hemp and other vegan materials. Don’t think hippie ponchos; think warm zip-ups, flowy skirts and soft tank tops.
